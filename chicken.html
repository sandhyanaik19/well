1. Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken
  • 2 Onions
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Chicken masala
  • Salt & oil

Steps

  1. Heat oil, fry onions until golden.
  2. Add tomatoes, ginger-garlic paste, and masala.
  3. Add chicken and cook until soft.
  4. Serve hot with rice or chapati.

2. Chicken Fry

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken
  • Red chilli powder
  • Turmeric
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt & oil

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with masala and lemon.
  2. Heat oil and fry until brown.
  3. Serve hot.

3. Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken
  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • Biryani masala
  • Curd
  • Onion & tomato

Steps

  1. Cook rice separately.
  2. Prepare chicken gravy with spices.
  3. Layer rice + chicken and cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Serve hot.

4. Chicken Kabab

Ingredients

  • Boneless chicken
  • Curd
  • Red chilli powder
  • Garam masala
  • Salt & oil

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken in curd + masala for 1 hour.
  2. Pan fry or grill the pieces.
  3. Serve with mint chutney.

5. Chicken Soup

Ingredients

  • 200g Chicken
  • Onion
  • Ginger
  • Pepper powder
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil chicken with onion and ginger.
  2. Add pepper and salt.
  3. Serve warm.
