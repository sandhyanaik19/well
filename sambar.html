1. Onion Sambar

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Toor dal
  • Small onions (sambar onions)
  • Tamarind water
  • Tomato
  • Sambar powder
  • Turmeric
  • Salt
  • Mustard seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • Red chilli

Steps

  1. Cook toor dal with turmeric until soft.
  2. Heat oil and fry small onions until golden.
  3. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Pour in tamarind water and boil for 5 minutes.
  5. Add sambar powder and salt.
  6. Mix cooked dal and boil for another 5–7 minutes.
  7. Prepare tadka with mustard, curry leaves, red chilli and add.
  8. Serve hot with rice or idli.

2. Hotel Style Sambar

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Toor dal
  • 1 small carrot
  • Drumstick
  • Potato (optional)
  • Tamarind
  • Jaggery (small piece)
  • Sambar powder
  • Turmeric
  • Salt
  • Mustard seeds
  • Coriander leaves
  • Hing (asafoetida)

Steps

  1. Cook toor dal until smooth and mash well.
  2. Boil vegetables with turmeric and salt.
  3. Add tamarind water and boil for 5 minutes.
  4. Add sambar powder and a small piece of jaggery.
  5. Mix dal into the boiling sambar and cook for 10 minutes.
  6. Prepare tadka with mustard + hing and add.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves.
  8. Serve with idli/dosa or rice.

3. Brinjal Sambar (Kathirikai Sambar)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Toor dal
  • 4–5 Small brinjals (cut into cubes)
  • 1 Tomato (chopped)
  • Small lemon-sized Tamarind
  • 1 tbsp Sambar powder
  • 1/4 tsp Turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • A pinch of Hing (asafoetida)
  • 2 tsp Oil
    • For Tempering:
  • 1 tsp Mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp Urad dal
  • 2 Dry red chillies
  • Few Curry leaves

Steps

  1. Pressure cook the toor dal with turmeric until soft and mash well.
  2. Soak tamarind in warm water and extract juice.
  3. Heat oil and sauté brinjal pieces for 3–4 minutes.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Pour in tamarind water and boil for 5 minutes.
  6. Add sambar powder, hing, and salt.
  7. When brinjal becomes soft, add the cooked dal and mix well.
  8. Boil the sambar for 8–10 minutes until thick and aromatic.
  9. Prepare tempering: heat oil, crackle mustard seeds, urad dal, red chilli, and curry leaves.
  10. Add tempering to the sambar and mix gently.
  11. Serve hot with rice, idli, or dosa.

4. Bele Sambar (Dal Sambar)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Toor dal
  • 1 Potato (cubed)
  • 1 Carrot (chopped)
  • 1 Tomato (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup Beans (cut)
  • Small lemon-sized Tamarind
  • 1 tbsp Sambar powder
  • 1/4 tsp Turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • A pinch of Hing (asafoetida)
  • 2 tsp Oil
    • For Tempering:
  • 1 tsp Mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp Urad dal
  • 2 Dry red chillies
  • Few Curry leaves

Steps

  1. Pressure cook toor dal with turmeric until soft. Mash well.
  2. Soak tamarind in warm water and extract the juice.
  3. Boil vegetables (potato, carrot, beans, tomato) with a little salt.
  4. Add sambar powder, hing, and tamarind water to the vegetables and boil for 5 minutes.
  5. Add mashed dal and mix well. Add water if needed and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. For tempering: heat oil, add mustard, urad dal, red chillies, and curry leaves.
  7. Pour tempering over the sambar and mix gently.
  8. Serve hot with rice, idli, or dosa.
