5 Easy Indian Roti Varieties

1. Chapati (Plain Roti)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • Water
  • Salt (optional)

Steps

  1. Knead flour with water into soft dough.
  2. Roll small balls into circles.
  3. Cook on tawa until both sides puff.

2. Phulka Roti

Ingredients

  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • Water
  • Salt (optional)

Steps

  1. Roll roti very thin.
  2. Cook on tawa slightly.
  3. Place on open flame → it will puff.

3. Paratha

Ingredients

  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • Oil or ghee
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Knead dough with salt.
  2. Roll slightly thick and add oil/ghee layers.
  3. Cook on tawa applying ghee.

4. Masala Roti

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Turmeric
  • Chilli powder
  • Coriander powder
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix spices with flour and knead dough.
  2. Roll into rotis.
  3. Cook on tawa with little oil.

5. Aloo Paratha

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Boiled potatoes
  • Chilli powder
  • Coriander powder
  • Salt
  • Ghee/oil

Steps

  1. Prepare spiced potato filling.
  2. Stuff inside dough ball and roll gently.
  3. Cook on tawa with ghee.
