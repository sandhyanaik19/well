1. Tomato Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Heat oil and fry onions.
  2. Add tomatoes and masala.
  3. Mix cooked rice and serve.

2. Lemon Rice

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Lemon
  • Mustard seeds
  • Curry leaves

Steps

  1. Heat oil and add mustard + curry leaves.
  2. Add turmeric and salt.
  3. Mix rice and squeeze lemon.

3. Upma

Ingredients

  • Rava (sooji)
  • Onion
  • Green chilli
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Roast rava and keep aside.
  2. Fry onions + chilli.
  3. Add water, salt, then rava.

4. Vegetable Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Bread slices
  • Tomato
  • Cucumber
  • Onion
  • Green chutney

Steps

  1. Apply chutney on bread slices.
  2. Place tomato, onion, and cucumber.
  3. Close sandwich and serve.

5. Masala Dosa

Ingredients

  • Dosa batter
  • Potatoes
  • Onion
  • Green chilli
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Make dosa batter thin on tawa.
  2. Prepare potato masala and spread in dosa.
  3. Fold dosa and serve hot.
